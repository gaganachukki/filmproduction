Our Expertise

Pre-Production

Script development, storyboarding, casting, location scouting, and comprehensive project budgeting to ensure a smooth shoot.

Principal Photography

Executing the shoot with industry-standard cinema cameras, specialized lighting rigs, drone cinematography, and expert directing.

Post-Production

Editing, color grading, visual effects (VFX), motion graphics, and mastering in our cutting-edge editing suites.

Audio & Sound Design

Foley, ADR, custom scoring, and Dolby Atmos surround sound mixing to create an immersive auditory experience.

Studio Rentals

Access to our versatile soundstages featuring green screens, pre-built sets, and dedicated changing rooms.

Equipment Rental

Need gear? We rent out top-tier ARRI, RED, and Sony cinema cameras, along with high-end Cooke and Zeiss lenses.
